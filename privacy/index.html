Privacy Policy

Effective March 2025

Requiem does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

§1 Data Processing

All HTTP requests and responses are constructed and processed locally on your device. Requiem does not route traffic through intermediary servers. When you send a request, it goes directly from your device to the destination URL.

§2 File Storage

.http files are stored using your device's local file system. Requiem does not access files beyond those you explicitly open in the app.

§3 Sharing

When you create a req.to link, the request content is encoded in the URL fragment. Fragment data is not sent to a server — it is decoded locally by the recipient's browser or app. Shared .http files are handled by the system Share Sheet.

§4 Analytics & Tracking

Requiem does not include analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking mechanisms of any kind.

§5 Third-Party Services

Requiem does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

§6 Contact

For privacy questions: support@x-amz.dev
